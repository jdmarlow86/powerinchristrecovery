Chapters

Launch and sustain Christ-centered recovery in your community.

Whether you're discerning your first gathering or strengthening an existing group, this guide provides the prayerful steps, tools, and connections needed to host a Power in Christ Recovery chapter.

Begin with Prayerful Preparation

Seek the Spirit's leading, invite partners, and shape a safe place to heal.

Every chapter begins with a surrendered heart. Start by gathering a prayer team, listening to the needs of your community, and discerning the leaders who can host, mentor, and shepherd the group. The steps below outline a proven rhythm our chapters use to stay rooted in Christ while serving with excellence.

Start a Chapter

Bring the Power in Christ Recovery journey to your city or church.

Launching a new chapter begins with prayer, preparation, and a heart to serve. Use these steps to establish a grace-filled recovery community where you are:

  • Pray & Discern: Gather a small team committed to interceding for your community.
  • Secure a Location: Partner with a local church or ministry space that can host weekly meetings.
  • Equip Your Team: Review the Power in Christ 10-Step resources and identify leaders for prayer, hospitality, and follow-up.
  • Share the Vision: Invite pastors, mentors, and potential participants to learn about the program and its Christ-centered approach.
  • Plan the Launch: Schedule an interest night, outline promotion, and map the first month of gatherings.
